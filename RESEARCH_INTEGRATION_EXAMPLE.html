Abstract of Long-Term Scientific Direction

My research trajectory is grounded in systems-level thinking applied to three interconnected domains: artificial intelligence architecture, biomedical innovation through quantum-inspired approaches, and human-machine integration. The overarching goal is to advance toward artificial general intelligence while ensuring safety and alignment, explore revolutionary therapeutic pathways via quantum computing principles, and develop seamless brain-computer interfaces that augment human cognition. These directions converge on a singular purpose: building intelligent systems that solve humanity's most pressing challenges while maintaining rigorous scientific discipline and ethical accountability.
